Frequently Asked Questions

When should I start looking for packers movers? ⮟

It all depends on the way you are shifting. Be it local shifting within the same city, then you should start looking for them at least 4-8 days prior. And you need to search for a mover and packer 7-14 days prior when you are shifting outside the city. You should also need to begin your search a bit earlier if the moving date is supposedly the end of the month.

How to find the best packers and movers service? ⮟

You will have to share all the requirements for your shifting and we would share a tentative quote and will schedule a visit at your location to further evaluate the movement. Post understanding our estimated services, you can speak to our moving team experts and then finalize the deal to go ahead.

How can I reduce my shifting charges? ⮟

The quantity and the type of goods you are moving is the primary factor that affects the expense of relocation. You can reduce the charges if you can reduce the number of items through decluttering. You also need to plan the move in advance and be sure to move on the correct day.

What are the things packers movers will take care of? ⮟

From the beginning till the end every process will be properly taken care of by the relocation experts. It includes decluttering, packing, loading, transportation, unloading, unpacking, and installation. When it comes to domestic relocation that is outside of the city the charges for unpacking and other services may not be included. However, inclusion is made on-demand.

How much time it will take to complete the move? ⮟

The normal time consumption would be 5-10 hours depending on the number of items as well as the floor of the house while relocation is taking place in the locality. 4-8 days would be the time consumption while shifting to the outskirts depending on the distance from the origin city to the availability of the required transportation.

What is a pre-move survey, and how much time does it take? ⮟

The quantity of the goods along with the time, pick up and the demands for delivery, are the requirements for relocation in pre-move packers and movers. Survey inspectors would arrive at your place and it usually takes 10-20 minutes, however, you can shift a few itemsover the call as well.

My family wishes to get our car loaded in our presence. Is it feasible? ⮟

Yes, RePorter values its clients and their precious belongings. We have GPS-enabled vehicle carriers to load the car in your presence and similarly deliver it to your new destination.

How should I believe we will get satisfactory services at the destination of deport? ⮟

RePorter is a well-established Pan India company, offering moving services in key locations and cities. We have light to heavy modern transport carriers, shifting/transporting equipment, all-weather strong packing, well-trained manpower. We observe complete safety measures and have proper means, technique and knowledge to handle almost everything from a tiny piece of a pin to the heavy machinery. Our main motto is to deliver the utmost satisfaction to our widespread clientele all over India.

I am worried about my perishable food items. How is RePorter going to handle and preserve the same? ⮟

RePorter advises the clients not to shift fast-perishable and food items such as Vegetables, Fruits, Flowers, Ghee, Pickles, etc. as these may spoil your other valuable items. However, we do make arrangements for shifting grocery household items in sealed boxes as per the requirements of the clients.

Can you hold the consignment at the destination for a week, if so what are the charges.? ⮟

Yes, we can do this. The visiting officer will address the questions.

How much should I tip the shifting labor? ⮟

In TamilNadu, the labor wages are quite low so on the terms of humanity appreciate them with your tip say Rs 25- Rs 50/hour if they have done a skillful job.
